Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

Welcome to Streamku Player. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our application.

1. About the Application

Streamku Player is an IPTV player application that allows you to play your favorite channel content through URLs or playlist files you provide. This application is designed to provide a simple and convenient viewing experience.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, Streamku Player is committed to data minimization:

  • No Login Required: You do not need to create an account or sign in to use this application.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identity data.
  • No Location Tracking: Our application does not access or track your device's GPS location.
  • No Camera Access: We do not request permission to use or access your device's camera.
  • No Audio Access: We do not use the microphone or record audio from your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertisements)

This application includes advertisements to keep the service free of charge. We use third-party advertising providers (such as Google AdMob or Start.io) that may collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device Advertising ID.
  • Device technical information (model, OS version).
  • Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions).

This data is used solely for advertising and analytics purposes by the respective service providers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

While we do not collect personal data directly, we prioritize the security of our application to ensure a safe user experience free from harmful technical interference.

5. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated by updating this page.
